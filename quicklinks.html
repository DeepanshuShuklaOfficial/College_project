Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. Is accommodation included in the package?

Yes, comfortable stays in guest houses or hotels are included, depending on the destination.

2. Is food included in the tour?

Typically, breakfast is included. Other meals may be chargeable or self-arranged based on the itinerary.

3. Are transportation services provided?

Yes, local transportation and pick-up/drop services from major cities are usually included.

4. Are flights or train tickets included in the package?

No, airfare or train fare is generally not included and must be booked separately.

5. Are entry fees to monuments or attractions included?

No, most entry fees need to be paid directly at the location unless stated otherwise.

6. Are guided tours included?

Yes, professional guides are available for sightseeing and local experiences.

7. Is the tour safe for solo travelers or families?

Yes, safety is a priority, and the tours are designed for all types of travelers.
